## Full Description (4,000 chars max)

Stop leaving money on the table.

The average self-employed business owner overpays their taxes by $10,000–$100,000+ every single year — not because they cheat, but because nobody told them the legal strategies that high earners use every tax season. More Simple Tax changes that.

Built with credentialed tax professional Theia Willis (CTEC #A123456), the app walks you through 10 proven, IRS-legal tax strategies with live savings calculators — so you can see exactly what each move is worth before you do it.

── WHAT'S INSIDE ──

✦ S-Corp Election Calculator
Find out if switching to an S-Corp could save you $10K–$30K in self-employment taxes annually.

✦ Solo 401(k) Optimizer
Maximize retirement contributions and slash your taxable income at the same time.

✦ QBI Deduction Maximizer
Take 20% off your qualified business income — most business owners miss this completely.

✦ Home Office Deduction
Calculate your deduction correctly (not the way that triggers audits).

✦ Vehicle Deduction Tracker
Mileage vs. actual expense — the app tells you which method wins for your situation.

✦ HSA Strategy Planner
Triple tax advantage. Most people use maybe one of them.

✦ Hire Your Kids (Legally)
Pay your children up to $14,600/year tax-free and deduct it as a business expense.

✦ Augusta Rule
Rent your home to your business for up to 14 days/year — the IRS makes it tax-free income.

✦ Accountable Plan Builder
Reimburse yourself for legitimate business expenses through your company, not your personal account.

✦ Tax-Loss Harvesting
Offset investment gains and reduce your tax bill before December 31st.
